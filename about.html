About Us

Our passion for good old solid cars that make people's hearts skip a beat is the driving force behind everything we do. Exactly what a vintage car is might be a matter of opinion, but you know the ones that make you turn around in the street and remember the days when things were built to stand the test of time. Over the years, we've built up a collection of such beauties, and we take pride in finding new loving homes for them where they can be preserved for future generations.

Whether you have a soft spot for American muscle cars, British style fit for a king, Italian performance rides, or all of the above, we have something for you. You are welcome to stop by our showroom to check out the legends currently in stock, and we look forward to seeing you there. If you spot exactly what you want in our online store, you can place an order today and have your dream car delivered to your home within weeks, thanks to our highly dependable shipping partners.
